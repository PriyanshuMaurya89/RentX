Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about renting on RentX

How do I rent an item on RentX?
Renting an item is simple! Just browse our categories, select the item you need, choose your rental dates, and make a secure payment. The lender will then coordinate delivery or pickup with you.
What happens if the item gets damaged?
All rentals include basic protection against accidental damage. For expensive items, we recommend purchasing additional protection during checkout. In case of damage, report it immediately and our support team will assist with the resolution process.
How are payments handled?
Payments are processed securely through our platform. The renter's payment is held until 24 hours after the rental period ends to ensure everything went smoothly. Only then is the amount released to the lender, minus our service fee.
Can I extend my rental period?
Yes! If the item is available for extended periods, you can request an extension through the app. The lender will be notified and can approve your request. Additional charges will apply for the extended period.
How do I become a lender?
To become a lender, simply create an account, verify your identity, and list your items for rent. You'll need to provide clear photos, a detailed description, set your availability and pricing. Our team may review your listing before it goes live.
